Spanish Tofu Scramble

January, 9 2023

Serving: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 2 tbl spoons of neutral oil, such as grape seed or avocados
  • 1/2 red, white or yellow onion
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, diced small
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 4-6 tortillas
  • 1/2 tomatoes

Instructions

Take the tofu and lightly press out the water with both hands. This can also be achieved by Starting with a hot pan, add oil. Starting with a hot pan, add oil.
