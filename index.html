Hola!

Hi! My name is Eliana Vasquez (she/her/ella) and I'm from Riverside California! I enjoy learning new hobbies and a couple of my favorites are sewing, painting, photography, bullet journaling, and a few others. I love to combine my culture within all aspects of my life and especially my art. I gain most of my inspiration from visiting my family and friends in Mexico. During these trips I explore new pueblos mágicos, eat delicious food and make new memories with friends. Once back in Washington, I am obsessed with my cats (Moo Moo and Lunis) and love to play and stare at them for hours. I’m also a design student at Western Washington University in my final year. Which means that I’m super nervous and excited about what the future holds for me.
